# ⚡ SLR Energy Marketplace

A decentralized renewable energy trading platform built on blockchain, enabling individuals and communities to buy, sell, and auction surplus energy in a transparent, secure, and trustless environment.

## 🌍 Overview

SLR Energy Marketplace empowers prosumers (energy producers + consumers) to directly trade renewable energy such as solar and wind power without relying on centralized utilities. By leveraging blockchain technology, every transaction is immutable, verifiable, and transparent.

The platform introduces a token-based economy using **SLR Token**, ensuring seamless peer-to-peer energy exchange, real-time settlements, and decentralized ownership.

## 🚀 Key Features

* 🔗 **Blockchain-Based Transactions** – Secure, transparent, and immutable energy trading
* ⚡ **Peer-to-Peer Marketplace** – Direct buying and selling of surplus renewable energy
* 💰 **Auction System** – Dynamic pricing through real-time energy bidding
* 🪙 **SLR Token Integration** – Native token for payments, rewards, and governance
* 📊 **Real-Time Tracking** – Monitor production, consumption, and transaction history


## 🏗️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** React / Node.js
* **Blockchain:** Ethereum / Polygon
* **Smart Contracts:** Solidity
* **Backend:** Node.js (for analytics & IoT integration)
* **Storage:** Firebase

## 🔄 How It Works

1. Producers generate renewable energy (e.g., solar panels)
2. Surplus energy is tokenized and listed on the marketplace
3. Buyers can purchase directly or participate in auctions
4. Smart contracts handle transactions and ownership transfer
5. All records are stored on blockchain for full transparency

## 🎯 Vision

To create a decentralized energy ecosystem where individuals have full control over energy production, distribution, and monetization—driving sustainability and reducing dependence on centralized power systems.
